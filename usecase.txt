@startuml
actor GamePlayer
actor GameDeveloper
actor GameTicker

GamePlayer -up-> (Play game) 
GameTicker --> (Play game) :ticks
(Play game) .up.> (choice of level): include
(Play game) .right.> (choice of game's mode ): include
(Play game) .left.> (Signing in ): include


GamePlayer -> (Save game)
GamePlayer -right-> (Exit game)

GamePlayer -> (Chatting)
(Chatting) .down.> (Insert smiles ): extend
(Chatting) .down.> (Asking for developer help): extend

'More shop options
GamePlayer --> (Buy)

(Buy) ..> (Buy skins for Bman) :include
(Buy) ..> (Buy in-game money for real money) :include
(Buy) ..> (Buy smiles for chat) :include

GameDeveloper -up-> (View statistic of game)

GameDeveloper -down-> (Change game)
(Change game) -down-> (Adding new levels)
(Change game) -right-> (Adding in-game bonuses)
(Change game) -left-> (Changing level's difficulty)

GameDeveloper -right-> (View gamePlayers) 
(View gamePlayers) .right.>  (Ban of GamePlayer): exclude
(View gamePlayers) .down.>  (Unban of GamePlayer): exclude

'Adding additional shop functional to developer
GameDeveloper --> (Shop item creation)

(Shop item creation) ..> (Edit and add skins for Bman) :include
(Shop item creation) ..> (Edit and add smiles for chat) :include

GameDeveloper -left-> (Get money from game)

@enduml
